# CHELSEA

## what a terrible neighborhood
everything in chelsea made me want to die. i lived on 13th street and 8th avenue overlooking a gas station that sold some of the worst weed i have ever smoked. our apartment was under 220 square feet but i thought i could live "japanese style" there. some nights i would stay up blasting music in headphones getting fucked up while sitora slept. when i told her i slept with a girl in japan she promised she would kill herself and moved in. we would black out and fight over menial things and began a cycle of aggression and closeness that i later learned was an abusive pattern. i ran down the street one time to get away and she found me two hours later with my head on the cigar bar talking to strangers. doing all of this while everyone around you is wearing vineyard vines and boho chic is a very strange experience. sometimes i pray for her and her family
