# ATTACHMENT

## sometimes you want to hold on
countless times in my life i would take long walks in the rain or sit staring outside of my window looking at the rain, wearing headphones and doing poppers listening to one really good song over and over while my girlfriend slept. attachment can be very strong, especially when you want life to mean something. walking through kyoto listening to the taps on my umbrella, feeling so happy that my life's work of language study and applying myself to finally reach the summit where i can comfortably move about a country with a high barrier to entry, i always feel so happy. i try to take a picture but i can never save the feeling. still, it is nice to get outside
