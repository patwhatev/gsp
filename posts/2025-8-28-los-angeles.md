## henry flew me out
he used to put me up in a hotel and i wouldn't hear from him for days. we were supposed to be working but he'd be getting high or doing whatever and we'd finally meet up on the last day. now that he's sober i get to stay at his house and his dog slept in my bed the last few nights. it's always inspiring to hang with a friend that takes full advantage of their abilities and encourages you to do the same. i spent 8 minutes not breaking eye contact with a squirrel hoping that my circumstances would change
