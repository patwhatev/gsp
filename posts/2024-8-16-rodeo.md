# RODEO

## I went to the rodeo

We stayed at a horse ranch above the stables. I fed some of the horses peppermint and then we went to the next town over to see the rodeo. It was all the standard stuff - roping, bullriding, etc. After the rodeo we went to a small roadhouse where a wicken looking lady was leading karaoke with a live band. The bartender was a bodybuilder and she was also missing 4 fingers from her hand but she could still open bottles with it. After this trip my neck started hurting every day. I fell asleep on the picnic table outside
