## arjun broke his jaw
i visited a lot of people in the hospital around 2018. arjun had an accident fucking around with his phone while riding his bike. his jaw got wired shut and he ended up losing a lot of weight. i ate an apple during that visit. there was another girl who i knew on twitter who passed out waiting for the train and woke up with both of her feet missing. there were tubes sucking this flat yellow liquid out of her limbs. i gave her a book and we talked for a while. my girlfriend also collapsed at a bar and we got separated when the ambulance came. i had to search for her in the rain going from hospital to hospital because her name was spelled weird
